About

Hi, This is Pushpanathan R, from Tamilnadu, India. I'm excited to be exploring the intersections of technology, creativity, and empathy. As a passionate tech enthusiast, I've developed a strong interest in Linux and PCs, and I'm always eager to deepen my knowledge in these areas.

In addition to my technical skills, I have a deep appreciation for creative fields such as graphic design and communication arts. I'm inspired by the world of art and the creator economy, where innovative ideas are brought to life through technology.

When I'm not exploring the latest tech trends or honing my creative skills, you can find me immersed in Tamil music and cinema. This vibrant and diverse musical genre resonates deeply with me, and I believe that it embodies the spirit of innovation and creativity that drives me.

My ultimate goal is to use technology as a tool to develop art and empathize with people. By combining my technical expertise with my creative passions, I hope to create meaningful experiences that inspire and connect with others. I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations in this exciting and ever-evolving digital world.
